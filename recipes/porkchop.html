Porkchop recipe

This is a quick and easy recipe for pork in a flavorful honey-garlic sauce

Ingidients

  • 4 ounce of pork chops
  • Salt and freshly ground black peper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
